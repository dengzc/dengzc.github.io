    Job market paper

    • The Power of Information in Combating Pollution: evidence from monitors in Chinese cities with Sebastian Axbard We investigate the effect of an air pollution monitoring program in China. One additional monitor in the city resulted in a 2-3 percent reduction in air pollution measured using satellite data over three years. The program appear to be particularly effective when the control of information is sufficiently insulated from government influence.
